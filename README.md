# 🏥 Healthcare Analytics Dashboard – Power BI

An interactive Power BI dashboard built on a 55,500-row hospital records dataset, designed to give hospital administrators a single-page view of patient volume, doctor/hospital coverage, revenue, and clinical outcomes.

> 🎓 Built during my **Data Analytics training at Cetpa Infotech Pvt. Ltd., Noida**, as part of hands-on practice with Power BI dashboarding and data visualization.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This dashboard — titled **"Healthcare Report"** — analyzes patient admission data across multiple hospitals to answer key operational questions:

- How many patients, doctors, and hospitals are in the system?
- What does the patient load look like per doctor?
- What is the total billing/revenue generated?
- Which medical conditions, blood groups, and insurance providers are most common?
- How are patients distributed across admission types (Elective / Urgent / Emergency) and test results (Normal / Abnormal / Inconclusive)?

---

## 📊 Dataset

- **Rows:** 55,500 patient records
- **Columns:** Name, Age, Gender, Blood Type, Medical Condition, Date of Admission, Doctor, Hospital, Insurance Provider, Billing Amount, Room Number, Admission Type, Discharge Date, Medication, Test Results
- **Date Range:** May 2019 – May 2024
- **Format:** Excel (.xlsx)

---

## 🖥️ Dashboard Features

**KPI Cards**
- Total Patients
- Total Doctors
- Total Hospitals
- Total Revenue
- Avg. Patients per Doctor
- Avg. Age of Patient
